We value

Customer Orientation

Knowing how to help customers depends on your ability to empathize with their challenges. If we can know how clients feel, we can help them to feel better, which is the most important part of a customer service job. We see the satisfaction of our needs within the satisfaction of the Customer's needs.

Flexibility

We appreciate team members to adjust to short-term change quickly and calmly, so that you can deal with unexpected problems or tasks effectively. Streamlined workforces, shifts in technology and a changing market are just a few reasons why flexibility is the key value for our organization.

Result orientation

As a results-oriented company we figure out how to get the right requirements, no matter the challenges faced, and stay resolutely focused on the core principles of gaining alignment and achieving clarity. We also ensure that our projects and efforts are consistently focused on delivering value for the company.

Creativity

Creativity is the highest form of intelligence because it goes beyond knowledge recall and extends into knowledge creation. Being creative is always asking questions and finding new solutions to problems . To be more likely to experiment with ideas in the workplace, which can lead to new processes that benefit clients and team members.

Safety

We consider data privacy and security of customers not just as a risk management issue, but as a potential source of competitive advantage that is a central component of gaining trust and our corporate reputation.

What makes our workplace culture unique?

Variety of challenging projects

We believe trying new approaches and strategies makes one more creative. DesigningIT provides possibilities to work on challenging projects that help to grow and learn.

International experience

Diving into a cross-cultural team and learning as you go with openness, honesty, and transparency can be one of the quickest route to you to step outside your comfort zone, which opens up opportunities to develop new skills and have new experiences.

Freedom and Flexibility

We are for designing the choice of the workplace and glad to give freedom to choose what you work on, as well as how, when, and where you perform your work. It is a growing priority for us to provide a working culture that is friendly and flexible.
